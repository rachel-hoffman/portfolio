About Me


Hi there! I am currently an Experience Architecture student at Michigan State University. I am looking to use my knowledge in user experience to get into user-centered product design after my graduation in 2025.

I am also pursuing a minor in Environmental and Sustainability Studies. It is a great passion of mine to work towards a cleaner and heatherier world for all of us. Working for large companies where goods are being mass-produced every day, small design changes can have a monumental environmental impact. Understanding this concept, I want to ensure that when I am in the position of a designer at a large company, that I have the knowledge necessary to make sure me and my team are making the right decisions for the future of our world.
