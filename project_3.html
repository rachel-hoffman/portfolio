Logo Design

An Individual Graphic Design Project


Background

This project will delve into my individual design process, including the research and thought processes that led me to a final logo design. As part of MSU’s graphic design program, I was tasked with redesigning a logo for a nonprofit or charity organization using the software Adobe Illustrator. Being a fan of the Awesome Socks Club, I decided to represent the nonprofit to which this company donates 100% of its profits: Partners in Health.
